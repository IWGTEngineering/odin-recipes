BBQ Beans

Description

BBQ beans were a staple food of mine while I was a student and working from home. Simple and filling.

Ingredients:

  • A tin of baked beans
  • BBQ Sauce
  • (Optional) Cheddar Cheese

Steps:

  1. (Optional) Heat the beans as instructed on the tin
  2. Add a large squirt of BBQ sauce and stir
  3. (Optional) Grate Cheddar Cheese over the bowl
